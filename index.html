Looking to jump start your CS career? Trying to boost your resume before career fair and future hackathons?

Look no further! HackGT presents HackGTeeny, a series of beginner workshops to introduce critical skills for application development. Hosted by Georgia Tech's favorite hackathon, we're offering a 5 hour, hands-on, mentor-guided grind for students with some (but not extensive) coding experience.

Frequently Asked Questions

  • Who is eligible?

    As long as you're a current Georgia Tech student, you're eligible. No prior experience is required! Just register!

  • What should I bring?

    Bring your laptop (and charger!), headphones, and anything else you need to work comfortably. We'll supply lunch. If you're still not sure, ask us on Facebook or email!

  • Where do I go?

    HackGTeeny will take place in the CULC in lecture halls 144 and 152.
